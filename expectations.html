What is expected from students?

  • Take responsibility for your own learning! Teachers open the door, but YOU have to enter by yourself!!
  • ASK when you don’t understand! We will find out together if necessary!
  • Work diligently and hard.
  • Set daily goals for yourself and stick to them. Work at home if you don’t finish at school. Make use of resources like the internet/library to help you master concepts that you struggle with.
  • Study work regularly and make notes when the subject specialists are there. Ask if you need help.
  • Assess your work regularly and make corrections. Use sticky notes to mark concepts that you do not understand. Ask the subject specialists to explain these concepts when they come to class.
  • Please ask if you need help with time management, setting goals, etc.!
  • It will be our privilege and an honour to guide you along this learning pathway!
  • Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any more questions!
  • Be self-motivated!
