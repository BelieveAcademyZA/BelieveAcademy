Selection of subjects (IGCSE)

Compulsory: English First Language, Afrikaans Second Language, Mathematics, and any Science Subject.

Choices: Two additional subjects

The duration of the IGCSE is usually two years.

Examination Rule: Examinations of all subjects must be completed within two calendar years.

Matriculation Exemption

The duration of the AS level is one year, and the A level can be completed in an additional year.

Three alternative outcomes are possible if the IGCSE choices conform to the rules mentioned.

Option 1: Pass four subjects on the AS level (at least 50%) and one on the IGCSE level (at least 60%).

Examination Rule: This option includes the rule that your IGCSE subject and your AS subjects must be completed within two calendar years.

Option 2: Pass three subjects on the A level (at least 40%) and two on the IGCSE level (at least 60%).

Option 3: Pass two subjects on the A level (at least 40%) and three on the IGCSE level (at least 60%).
