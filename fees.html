Fees

Once-off registration fee per family (development of center)
IGCSE R1 500 GED R750

Fees may be paid in three different ways:

  • One payment per year (10% discount - pay before the 1st of January of the year of tuition).
  • Twelve payments per year.
  • Ten payments per year.
All payments must be made before the first of the month.
Interruption of payment will require reregistration.
