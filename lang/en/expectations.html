Expectations

This statement emphasises the importance of student responsibility in the learning process and encourages proactive engagement with education.

It serves as a motivational and instructional guide, setting clear expectations for students while fostering a supportive learning environment. It emphasizes the partnership between teachers and students in pursuing knowledge and personal growth.

STUDENTS:

  1. Personal Responsibility:
    • Students are encouraged to take responsibility for their own learning journey and ownership of their education.
  2. Work Ethic:
    • Tutors advocate diligence and hard work in the learning process.
    • Students are encouraged to set and stick to daily goals.
  3. Resource and Study Habits:
    • Students are advised to use various resources, including the internet and the library.
    • Independent study methods are encouraged to master challenging concepts.
    • Seek help from subject specialists when needed.
  4. Continuous Assessment:
    • Self-assess and correct their work.
    • Use sticky notes to mark unclear concepts for clarification.

TUTORS:

  1. Active Communication:
    • Tutors are facilitators who open doors to knowledge and encourage learners to engage actively with the learning process.
  2. Support and Guidance:
    • Tutors invite students to seek help with time management and goal setting.
    • They are committed to guide students on their learning pathway.
    • Do not hesitate to ask questions so the tutors can address any misunderstanding.
